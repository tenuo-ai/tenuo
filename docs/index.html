How it works

Three steps from issuance to enforcement

1

Issue

Control Plane mints a signed root warrant defining the maximum scope of authority.

2

Attenuate

Orchestrator restricts capabilities and binds the warrant exclusively to the Worker Agent's public key.

3

Verify

Worker presents the chain. Gateway/sidecar verifies locally in microseconds.
